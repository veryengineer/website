Open & collaborative projects

Have you ever had an idea that you just knew would help the world? And you got stuck because you couldn't do it on your own, and abandoned it? Welcome to the club.

The problem is often that we give up. And we shouldn't. Because even if we're facing a hard problem, there is always someone out there that wants to help. People are awesome like that.

This initiative is dedicated to all the future ideas that will see the light of day and make a change, because we didn't quit. By doing public pilots we will together find a means to help engineers to make their dreams come true. Yes, finding support may seem scary or impossible sometimes; we are going to change that and make it happen, together.

First pilot

A first pilot aims to design a solar powered scrypt miner to reduce the footprint of cryptocurrency mining for Dogecoin, Litecoin and Digibyte. It will start with a series of introductions across Github, Twitter and Reddit. Once there is sufficient interest, design workshops can start using an open, interactive collaborative platforms.

Don't be afraid and join us to be part of something new. Who knows... it may be the next big thing.

Announcements will follow.
